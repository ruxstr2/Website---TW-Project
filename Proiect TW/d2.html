Mozaic Baths (The Temple District)


Why 'baths'? One should rather ask why 'mozaic', since that first word is key to understanding the veraynzi way of embracing disparate cultures, thus creating a marvellous picture of unique, yet equally beautiful fragments.


The city's main temple district can be summed up using only a few precious words.
Granted, some say that they make no sense, but have only been chosen because they provide an entertaining rhyme.


True to the district's inclination towards inexcusable opulence, stands the Grand Temple, once housing Council Meetings
